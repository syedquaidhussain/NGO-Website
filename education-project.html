Our Education Projects

1. URDU ACADEMY

NGO Great Samaj Sevi Sanstha is committed for the Empowerment of Women through Education as a result has partnered with Government of Uttar Pradesh (Urdu Academy) to educate women in underserved areas and link them with main stream education and social empowerment. Presently NGO is providing academic opportunities of learning and growth to more than 45 women in Shahjahanpur through the support from Urdu Academy, Uttar Pradesh.

2. Child Labour & Crèche Program

Great Samaj Sevi Sanstha with aim & objective to eliminate child labor had started Education Program for the children living in vulnerable conditions & it is supported through a crèche program with life skills followed by infotainment to the children so that the interest for the learning remains in their mind & they love the education given to them for their mainstreaming.
